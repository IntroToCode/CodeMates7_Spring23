# Morning Waffle

## Servings: 
- The serving size is 4
## Time to prepare: 
- This will take 25 minutes to prepare.

## Ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- Waffles mix
- Maple syrup

## Equipment needed:
- Bowl
- Skillet
- Mixer
- Plate

## Steps for preparation:
- Mix the eggs and waffes using the bowl
- Heat the skillet
- Add the mixture in the skillet
- Wait for 5 minutes
- Put in a plate
- Add syrup
- Enjoy
